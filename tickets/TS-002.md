# Ticket TS-002

**Date Opened:** 2026-01-20  
**Issue Category:** Performance Degradation  
**Customer Type:** End User  
**Product Type:** Gas-Powered Equipment  
**Priority Level:** Medium  

## Problem Description
Customer reports reduced cutting performance and intermittent stalling during normal operation.

## Environment / Context
Equipment used for residential yard maintenance. Unit has not received recent servicing. Fuel reported to be from an older container.

## Troubleshooting Steps Taken
1. Confirmed symptoms during operation
2. Inspected air filter for blockage
3. Checked fuel quality and fuel lines
4. Verified choke and throttle operation

## Resolution
Air filter was heavily clogged and fuel was stale. Air filter was replaced and fresh fuel was added. Equipment operated normally after service.

## Root Cause
Restricted airflow and degraded fuel quality causing inefficient combustion.

## Preventive Recommendation
Advise regular air filter inspection and replacement per maintenance schedule and use of fresh fuel to maintain optimal performance.
