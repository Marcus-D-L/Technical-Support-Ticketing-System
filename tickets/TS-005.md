# Ticket TS-005

**Date Opened:** 2026-01-20  
**Issue Category:** Mechanical / Safety Interlock  
**Customer Type:** Dealer  
**Product Type:** Gas-Powered Equipment  
**Priority Level:** High  

## Problem Description
Dealer reports equipment shuts off immediately after startup, preventing normal operation.

## Environment / Context
Equipment recently serviced. Safety features engaged during startup sequence. No error codes displayed.

## Troubleshooting Steps Taken
1. Verified safety interlock and operator presence controls
2. Inspected safety switch alignment
3. Checked for obstructions or misaligned components
4. Tested startup sequence after adjustment

## Resolution
Safety switch was misaligned during servicing. Switch was repositioned and secured, restoring normal operation.

## Root Cause
Improper alignment of safety interlock switch following recent maintenance.

## Preventive Recommendation
Ensure safety interlock alignment is verified after servicing and before returning equipment to operation.
