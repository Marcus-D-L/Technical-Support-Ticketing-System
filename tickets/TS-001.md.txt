# Ticket TS-001

**Date Opened:** 2026-01-20  
**Issue Category:** Power / Startup Issue  
**Customer Type:** Dealer  
**Product Type:** Battery-Powered Equipment  
**Priority Level:** High  

## Problem Description
Dealer reports equipment will not power on even though the battery indicates a full charge.

## Environment / Context
Equipment stored in a garage environment. Ambient temperature approximately 40Â°F. No recent firmware updates reported.

## Troubleshooting Steps Taken
1. Verified battery charge level
2. Tested with a known-good battery
3. Inspected battery terminals for corrosion or debris
4. Checked safety switch engagement

## Resolution
Battery terminals were cleaned and reseated. Unit powered on and operated normally after corrective action.

## Root Cause
Poor electrical contact at battery terminals caused by debris buildup.

## Preventive Recommendation
Recommend routine inspection and cleaning of battery terminals during scheduled maintenance to prevent recurrence.
